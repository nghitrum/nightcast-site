Delete Your Account

We're sorry to see you go.

You can delete your NightCast account and all associated data at any time. Once deleted, this action cannot be undone.

FROM THE APP

The easiest way to delete your account is directly in the NightCast app:

  1. Open NightCast and go to the Profile tab
  2. Scroll to the bottom and tap Delete Account
  3. Confirm the deletion when prompted

VIA EMAIL

If you are unable to access the app, you can request account deletion by emailing us at support@nightcast.app with the subject line "Account Deletion Request". Please include the email address associated with your account. We will process your request within 48 hours.

WHAT GETS DELETED

When you delete your account, the following data is permanently removed from our servers:

  • Your profile information (email, name, avatar)
  • Your saved location data
  • Your notification preferences and history
  • Your event interaction history
  • Your push notification token

Note: If you have an active Pro subscription, deleting your account does not automatically cancel your subscription. Please cancel your subscription through the Google Play Store or Apple App Store before deleting your account to avoid future charges.

DATA NOT AFFECTED

The following data is stored only on your device and is not affected by account deletion:

  • Cached sky event data
  • Local notification history
  • App settings and preferences stored on-device

To remove this data, uninstall the NightCast app from your device.

QUESTIONS?

If you have questions about data deletion, please contact us at support@nightcast.app.
