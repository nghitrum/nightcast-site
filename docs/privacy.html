Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

NightCast is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your information.

WHAT WE COLLECT

Location

We use your location to calculate which sky events are visible from your position, including rise/set times, altitude, and visibility scores. Your location is stored on your device and in your account profile if you create one.

Email

If you create an account, we store your email address for authentication and account recovery.

Notification Preferences

We store your notification settings (lead time, minimum score, enabled event types) to deliver timely sky event alerts.

Camera

NightCast uses your device camera for the AR Sky Pointer feature, which overlays celestial objects on your live camera view to help you find them in the sky. Camera data is processed on-device in real time and is never recorded, stored, or transmitted.

Device Sensors

We use your device's compass and motion sensors to determine which direction you're pointing for the AR Sky Pointer and Sky Compass features. This data is processed on-device only.

Usage Data

We collect anonymous usage data such as which features you use and how you interact with the app. This helps us improve NightCast for everyone.

THIRD-PARTY SERVICES

NightCast uses the following third-party services to operate:

  • Supabase — authentication and database
  • RevenueCat — subscription management
  • Sentry — crash reporting and error tracking
  • Expo — push notifications
  • N2YO API — satellite pass predictions
  • Open-Meteo — weather data

Each of these services has its own privacy policy governing how they handle data.

YOUR DATA IS NOT FOR SALE

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal data to third parties. Your information is used solely to provide and improve the NightCast experience.

DATA RETENTION

Account data is kept for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, your data will be removed from our servers. Anonymous users' data is stored on-device only and is never uploaded to our servers.

CONTACT US

If you have questions about this privacy policy or your data, reach out to us at support@nightcast.app.
